Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our Subzero appliance repair services.

Why is my Subzero refrigerator not cooling?

Cooling issues are often caused by dirty condenser coils, faulty evaporator fans, or sealed system problems. Our technicians perform a full diagnostic to identify the exact cause.

Why is my Subzero ice maker not producing ice?

This can be caused by clogged water lines, faulty inlet valves, or sensor issues. We inspect water pressure, components, and electronics for a permanent fix.

Why is my Subzero wine cooler not cooling properly?

Temperature issues may result from evaporator fan problems, ice buildup, or thermostat failure. Cleaning and component replacement usually resolves this.
